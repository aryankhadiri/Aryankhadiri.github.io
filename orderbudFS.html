OrderBud

Final Project of CodePath iOS University Course
(In Progress)

Summary

We are developing this application as a team of 3 students in San Jose State University. I am the team leader and have presented the original Idea. This application is dedicated to the restaurants and cafes. This is meant to solve the problem of not knowing what to order and whether or not you will be satisfied with your choice. This application intends to give you some insights as well as images about the items in the menu so you can have a better idea of how it tastes.
